Over the course of four days, ATLS will feature:

Live Expo Showcasing Startups: Discover the latest innovations at our live expo, where Africa’s most promising startups showcase their solutions to investors and potential partners.

Workshops by Industry Experts: Enhance your skills through workshops led by top tech experts, covering the latest industry tools, trends, and strategies.
